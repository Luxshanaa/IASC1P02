Things to do in Ontario this Winter ❄️

Wondering how to spend the holidays this winter? Here's a list places you can check out this holiday season if you live in Ontario!

Distillery Winter Village

The Distillery Winter Village, is your go to place for cute holiday Instagram pictures, food and a fun day out with family and friends. The best part is the place offers free entry on weekdays before 4 PM EST on select days. Make sure to double check online before going to save yourself the hassle of buying tickets! For more details check out their website here.

Casa Loma's Castle and Holiday Light Tour

Another experience you wouldn’t want to miss is the Casa Loma Holiday Lights Tour! The beautiful castle has been decorated inside and out with Christmas decorations. They have a Santa's workshop and the Holiday Light Tour, where you can witness a winter wonderland around the circumferecne of the castle. Learn more on what they have to offer this holiday season here.

Canada's Wonderland WinterFest

Canada’s Wonderland’s annual WinterFest has fun events lined up for the holiday season. There are shows, attractions, rides and food! It’s also outdoors, which makes it more safe to practice Covid-19 guidelines while still enjoying the holidays with loved ones. Just make sure you’re bundled up and ready to have fun! View the events they have here.

Skating at Nathan Phillips Square

Nothing screams the Canadian holiday spirit like skating! What better way to spend this winter season skating at Nathan Philips Square enjoying the beautiful city view covered in festive decorations. Skates can also be rented there for a reasonable price and those who already have skates can also get them sharpened there for a small fee. For more details check out their website here.

Hope you find this list useful! Remember to spend this holiday season in a safe manner adhering to Covid-19 guidelines. Happy Holidays!
